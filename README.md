## üìà Key Features & Deliverables

This project involved the development and execution of robust analytical processes and scripts:

* **ETL Pipeline:** Developed and executed **advanced SQL queries** and **Python scripts** to clean, transform, and load raw data.
* **KPI Calculation:** Calculated core Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) including:
    * Monthly Orders & Revenue
    * Category-wise Revenue Contribution
    * Customer Retention Rates
    * Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
* **Statistical Analysis:** Performed **correlation analysis** to understand the relationship between different business metrics (e.g., price vs. quantity sold).
* **Trend Forecasting:** Used time-series analysis techniques to forecast future sales trends.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Applied segmentation techniques (e.g., RFM or K-means clustering) to pinpoint **top customers**.

## üß† Business Insights Gained

The analysis yielded critical insights that inform business strategy and operational adjustments:

* **Optimized Inventory/Product Strategy:** Identified **high-performing product categories** that drive the most revenue, suggesting focus areas for stocking and marketing.
* **Targeted Marketing Campaigns:** Pinpointed **top-value customers** and assessed **retention rates** to develop tailored loyalty programs and re-engagement strategies.
* **Strategic Growth Planning:** Calculated **YoY growth** and performed **trend forecasting** to set realistic sales targets and allocate resources effectively.
* **Vendor Management:** Visualized **seller-wise revenue contributions** to aid in strategic decisions regarding vendor partnerships and negotiations.
* **Data-Driven Decisions:** The correlation analysis provided a clear understanding of which factors (e.g., discounts, review scores) most heavily influence sales, guiding pricing and promotion strategies.
